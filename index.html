An Essential Oil For Anxiety


Some essential oils can be used as a natural way to reduce stress and anxiety. Lavender is one of the most well-known essential oils for anxiety and it smells very nice too. There are other essential oils that can help with stress and anxiety that aren't as popular as lavender.

Bergamot, Jasmine, Rose, and Chamomile are some other popular essential oils for stress and anxiety.
